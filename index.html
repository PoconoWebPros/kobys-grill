Our Story

Where Israel Meets
the Poconos

Nestled along a serene creek in Bartonsville, near Camelback Ski Resort and Great Wolf Lodge, Koby's Creekside Grill brings the bold flavors of Israeli and Mediterranean cuisine to the Pocono Mountains.

Chef Koby Ben brings a wealth of culinary expertise from Israel and New York City, specializing in farm-to-table dishes featuring prime aged butcher cuts — ribeye, skirt steaks — and grilled vegetables, all sourced from local farmers.

With outdoor seating overlooking the creek, a playground and soccer field for the kids, and live music events — it’s more than a meal, it’s an experience.
